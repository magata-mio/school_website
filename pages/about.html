    Welcome to D.P.Believers English School

    D.P.Believers English School is a co-educational English medium school based on sound Biblical values. Established in the year 2062 BS ( 2005 AD) DPBES is one of the first schools of Dharan-5, Deurali. . We inspire our students to love God , train and equip them to be life long learner, responsible, faithful and loyal citizens who will later serve God , community, the nation and the world at large. This school is administered and run by a group of extremely dedicated, experieced, highly qualified and ethusiastic academicians. We at DPBES belive that each child is gifted by God to achieve the purposes for which he/she has been created. In order to bring out the best in every child, the school family, parents/guardinas and the community must work together wholly and constantly. Therefore, at DPBES we work in harmony with parents to achive this objective. The school team strive to provide the finest child centered, quality education for the holistic development of each student. This school is recognized by Nepal Government and the medium of instruction is english at all levels. DPBES is open to all regardless of caste, creed, religion or faith.

    Our Mission

    To inspire every student to love God, equipping him/her to be lifelong learner, responsible, faithful and loyal citizen and to encourage each pupil to serve God, lead and transform his/her community and nation.

    Our Vision

    We envision equipping each student in every single way to realize his/her God-given purpose and guide everyone one to achieve it by collaborating with parents/guardians and teachers.

    Our Core Values

    Our goal for each student is to develop him/her holistically and see every child grow and live a well- balanced life; spiritually, physically, mentally, academically, athletically and socially.
